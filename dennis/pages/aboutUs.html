About Us

Our Story

Van Buren Funeral Services Limited is a limited Company, which was established in 1992 initially as a Funeral transport company and later as coffin manufacturing company. Van Buren Funeral Services Limited was established in the year 2001 to compliment the services of Van Buren Funeral Services Limited. In year 2007 Van Buren Funeral Services achieved their dream of opening ultra-modern funeral home which is situated along Diani way, Van Buren funeral home. These Van Buren Funeral Services have grown to be the most respected company in terms of having high quality hearses, coffins and services rendered to both local and international clients. We are renown for quality low cost and high cost coffins which we do not only supply locally but also to neighboring states.
