Serving Families with Dignity and Care

The loss of a loved one can leave you with a lot of unanswered questions, feelings of stress and anxiety and grief that makes events difficult to handle.
Our committed experienced team is with you at every step to help you from the very beginning of this complex process.
All of our funeral services in Nairobi, Kenya are designed to help you through one of the most personal and challenging stages of life. We understand that losing a loved one is an emotional and difficult experience, and are committed to providing compassionate care and professional and personal service.
You can count on us to help you plan a personal, lasting tribute to your loved one, and we will carefully guide you through the many decisions that must be made during this challenging time.
You are welcome to call us at any time of the day, any day of the week, for immediate assistance or visit our funeral home in Kenya at your convenience.
